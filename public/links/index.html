Mon, Jan 1, 0001

Some of my favourite Magic resources:

  • EDHrec: The first place you should go if you’re looking for inspiration for a new deck.

  • Scryfall: The definitive Magic Card Search engine. (Come check out my article on beginner scryfall syntax!)

  • Salubrious Snail’s Caluclators and Manabase Tool for checking my deck composition.

  • This article on building manabases for singleton formats, with a sweet calculator!

Some of my favourite Magic podcasts:

  • Listen to the Legendary Creature Podcast for sane, measured talk about the game of Commander.

  • I recommend going back and listening to the Commander Theory archive. It’s still one of the all time best.

  • I love Limited Level-Ups for learning more about improving as a limited player and deckbuilder.

  • Likewise for Rough Drafts. Especially if you want to learn more about hoe general Magic design and theory ideas apply to limited.
